Add Google Drive to Miro boards

Integrating Google Drive with Miro gives easy access to online resources.

Click on the thumbnails to expand the images below.

On your Miro board, click the three dots at the bottom of the left-hand tool bar. This will bring up the Apps pop-up.

Click on the Add more apps link in the top right section of the pop-up.

The Marketplace pop-up will open showing a selection of applications. From the listing in Featured Apps, click on the Google Drive by Miro card.

Activating the Google Drive card will display a 'Get app' button.

Click on the 'Get app' button and a pop-up on the Miro board will show that Google Drive has been installed.

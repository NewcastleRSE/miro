Setting up a Google Account

Setting up a Google account automatically creates a Gmail address and gives access to Google Cloud services. Google Drive is a sharable file storage service.

Click on the thumbnails to expand the images below.

Set up a Google Account at: https://accounts.google.com/signup

  1. Fill in your first and last name in the input boxes provided.
  2. Choose a username that will prefix '@gmail.com'.
  3. Create a password using 8 or more characters with a mix of letters, numbers & symbols.
  4. Confirm your password in the adjacent box.
  5. Click the 'Next' button.

Provide extra information to link to your account. An email address can be added to help if account recovery is needed.

  1. Add a mobile phone number to link to the account. (A text message will be sent)
  2. Add an email address for account recovery.
  3. Enter your date of birth and gender.
  4. Verify your phone number by clicking the 'Send' button.

Verify your phone number. Click on the 'Send' button to receive a text message with a 6 digit verification code.

Once the text message arrives with the verification code, enter it into the next form. Click the 'Verify' button.

There is an option to add extra account services at this stage. If this is not required 'Skip' can be selected.

Read the privacy and terms of the account.

Accept the terms and conditions by selecting the checkboxes at the bottom of the page for the Terms of Service and the Privacy Policy. Click 'Create Account'

Confirm your agreement by clicking the 'Confirm' button on the pop-up.

Your account has been created. View the Welcome page. A range of services are now available.

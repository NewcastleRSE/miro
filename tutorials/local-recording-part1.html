Set up Zoom to use local recordings - part 1

Part 1 - Set up the online Zoom portal

Set up an account with into Zoom at: https://zoom.us/

Sign into the account with your registered email and password.

Click on the Settings option on the left hand menu bar.

Click on the Recording tab in the main page section.

If Cloud Recording is selected, disable it by clicking on the right hand toggle button.

Confirm the change by selecting the Turn Off button on the pop-up.

Enable Local Recording by clicking on the right hand toggle button.

Confirm the change by selecting the Turn On button on the pop-up.

Sign out of the online Zoom portal by clicking on the avatar icon on the top-right of the page.
